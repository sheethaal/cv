ADVENTURE AWAITS



ADVENTURES OF CHOTA BHEEM

Experience XD MAX, a state-of-the-art ride that combines a 3D film with physical and environmental effects, in an air-conditioned theatre with 180 seats. Visitors will experience movement under their feet, splashes of water, gusts of wind and movie magic like never before. The 3D goggles will introduce the spectators to a new, larger-than-life world.

CATERPILLAR COASTER

Hop on board the Caterpillar coaster and train and chug along the termite country. Manoeuvre deft turns and up hills. The ride is a stepping stone for shy first timers and an appetiser for fun-junkies.

FIRE BRIGADE

Fire Brigade is a family ride with fully interactive one-of a-kind attraction that everyone is sure to love. Each of the Fire Brigade's six fire trucks feature seating in the cab for drivers as well as a rescue bucket where two brave future firefighters take aim with their water cannons to extinguish the fire raging through the ride's centrepiece building. This revolutionary family ride combines the best of classic circular car rides with a high tech, interactive water gun race game.

ROCKIN' TUG

The entire family can set sail for adventure on this innovative ride. Combining a unique mix of rocking and whirling motions, the Rocking Tug can accommodate up to 24 riders.

DASHING CARS 1 & 2

Your own formula - one circuit. Just pure fun. Dash and whack into each other. Sheer driving fun for all

DANCING WHEEL

Let us take you for a circular ride and on occasions it will take you up and down. Experience the delightful moments at the Dancing Wheel.

WONDER SPLASH

This ride is an absolute must for every guest visiting FUNTASTICA. A bogie that resembles a raft made of logs is hauled up a hill using a conveyor and taken through a tunnel. On emerging from the tunnel, it hurtles down a steep incline at a dizzying speed and splashes into a shallow pool, sending a glorious spray of water your way and drenching you in the process. This fun ride is suitable for all riders in all the age groups.
